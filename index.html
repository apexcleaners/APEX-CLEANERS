Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a cleaning?

Use our online booking form – choose your service, date, and time. It takes less than 2 minutes.

How do I pay?

We accept cash, Ecocash, and bank transfers. Payment is due after the service is completed – we never ask for money upfront.

Do you bring cleaning supplies?

Yes! We arrive fully equipped with professional tools and eco‑friendly products. You don't need to provide anything.

Can I cancel or reschedule?

Absolutely. Just let us know at least 12 hours before your appointment and we'll reschedule or cancel at no charge.

Do you clean offices?

Yes, we serve homes, apartments, offices, shops, and commercial properties. You can select the appropriate size when booking.

How can I track my booking?

After booking, you'll receive a unique Booking ID. Use our Track Booking page to see your status.

Is your team trustworthy?

Every cleaner is background‑checked, trained, and insured. Your safety and satisfaction are our top priority.

What if I'm not happy with the clean?

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not delighted, we'll return and re‑clean for free – no questions asked.
