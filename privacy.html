Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

Apex Cleaners ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website apexcleaners.github.io/APEX-CLEANERS. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information directly from you when you use our website:

  • Booking Form: Full name, email address, phone number, service preferences, and any special instructions you provide.
  • Review Form: Name, rating, and review text.
  • Quote Form: Name and email address.
  • Contact via WhatsApp/Email: Any information you choose to share when you contact us.

We also collect technical data automatically (such as browser type, IP address, and page interaction data) via Firebase services to improve our website.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data only for the following purposes:

  • To process and confirm your cleaning bookings.
  • To respond to your inquiries and quote requests.
  • To display approved reviews on our website.
  • To improve our website and services (using aggregated, non-personal data).
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your personal data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore database), which provides industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit. We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Bookings and reviews are kept in our database to provide and improve our service. You can request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 7).

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our website and business:

  • Firebase (Google) – for database, authentication, and analytics. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • EmailJS – to send booking confirmation emails. See EmailJS Privacy Policy.
  • Google Maps – to display our location (embedded map).
  • Tailwind CSS CDN – for website styling (no personal data is shared).

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties unless required by law.

6. Cookies

Our website may use essential cookies or similar technologies provided by Firebase for authentication and security purposes. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect the functionality of the website.

7. Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time (this will not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal).
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority if you believe your rights have been violated.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email or phone number below.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed at anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
